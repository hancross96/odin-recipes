Cheese omelette

Description

This cheese omelette is great for breakfast.

When cooking an omelette, it's important that you keep the temperature on the low side, as you don't want it to turn brown.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon milk, or as needed
  • Salt and ground white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Shredded Emmentaler cheese

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs in a bowl
  2. Add milk and season
  3. Melt butter over medium-low heat
  4. Pour in egg mixture and swirl
  5. Cook until egg starts to set, about 1 minute
  6. Carefully flip and cook until warmed through
  7. Sprinkle on cheese
